Project Overview

Why Choose Voice Memo Assistant?

01

Background & Motivation

Voice Memo Assistant was inspired by the limitations of traditional note-taking. Paper notes are hard to organize and retrieve, while existing apps often lack intelligent features. In a world where multitasking is common, people need a smarter, faster way to capture thoughts by using only their voice.

02

Problem Solving

This system addresses the lack of intelligent categorisation and the inconvenience of typing while record the memo. With speech-to-text, NLP classification, and smart categorisation, users can manage their memos entirely hands-free, especially in scenarios like driving or meetings.

03

Target Users

Voice Memo Assistant system is designed for students, office workers, and multitaskers. It benefits anyone who prefers voice input over typing—whether you're revising for exams, managing work meetings, or simply recording tasks in different domains.
